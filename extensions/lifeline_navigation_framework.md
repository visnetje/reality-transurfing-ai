# Life Line Navigation Framework

Version: 1.0

Purpose:
This module helps the AI explain how individuals move between life trajectories within the "space of variations".

Concept Definition:

Reality Transurfing proposes that all possible scenarios exist in the space of variations.

Each possible scenario corresponds to a "life line".

Individuals move through these life lines based on perception, intention, and energy balance.

Life Line Characteristics:

Life lines differ in:

* emotional atmosphere
* opportunities available
* relationships encountered
* overall life direction

Shift Mechanisms:

Movement between life lines may occur through:

1. Change of perception
2. Reduction of excess importance
3. Release from pendulum influence
4. Alignment between mind and soul

Key Navigation Factors:

Attention
Where attention goes, energy flows.

Importance
Exaggerated importance traps individuals in unfavorable trajectories.

Slides
Mental imagery influences the life line that becomes activated.

Types of Slides:

Negative Slides
Fear-driven scenarios repeatedly imagined.

Positive Slides
Constructive imagery aligned with desired states.

Mind–Soul Alignment:

When the rational mind and intuitive soul agree on a direction:

* tension decreases
* clarity increases
* decisions feel natural

This state allows smoother transition to compatible life lines.

Interpretation Framework:

When analyzing user situations, the AI may:

1. Identify current emotional atmosphere of the life line.
2. Detect excess importance locking the user into it.
3. Explore how perception or intention might shift trajectories.
4. Relate the process to Transurfing principles.

Important Constraint:

Life lines should be presented as a philosophical concept rather than a scientifically established phenomenon.
